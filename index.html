Background
Who I work with and what I’ve built before.

I’ve spent my career leading QA and delivery for complex products: billing, telecom, fintech and large-scale integrations.

I’ve led distributed teams of 200+ people across Japan, India, Europe and LATAM. I build QA and release processes for companies where downtime and bad releases are expensive – and where “just test more” is not a strategy.


  • I build QA practices that reduce delivery friction and make product releases more predictable. The emphasis is on clear ownership, tighter feedback loops, and removing unnecessary steps that slow down teams.
  • Most of my work involves products with complex integrations and cross-system impact. I help teams create visibility into these dependencies so issues surface earlier and require less rework.
  • In pre-production and integration testing stages, I focus on shortening cycle times by improving scenario relevance and eliminating low-value testing. The goal is to reach release readiness with fewer surprises and lower operational cost.
  • AI is applied where it removes manual bottlenecks — triage, log review, and repetitive scenario design. The result is faster analysis, more consistent quality decisions, and reduced load on senior engineers.
